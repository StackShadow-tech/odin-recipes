Cheese Pizza

Description

You will skip ordering take-out and make this delicious homemade Cheese Pizza this weekend.

If you want a perfect pizza night then you came to the right place and only will need a handful of ingredients that will give you the perfect Cheese Pizza!

ingredients

  • Pizza sauce
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. On a lightly floured surface, stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a non-stick baking sheet.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella all over the pizza.
  4. Sprinkle with parmesan before baking for 12 to 14 minutes, or until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbling.
